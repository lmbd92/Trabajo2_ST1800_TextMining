@@13874514 The Center for Housing Policy , the research affiliate of the National Housing Conference ( NHC ) , works to broaden understanding of the nations housing challenges and to examine the impact of policies and programs developed to address these needs . Combining research and practical , real-world expertise , the Center helps to develop effective policy solutions at the local , state and national levels that increase the availability of affordable homes . Jeffrey Lubell is the executive director of the Center . John McIlwain , senior resident fellow at the Urban Land Institute and ULI/J . Ronald Terwilliger chair for housing , is chairman of the Center . Maureen Friar is president and CEO of NHC . Dan Nissenbaum , chief operating officer of the Urban Investment Group , a division of Goldman Sachs Bank USA , is chair of NHC. # History # The Center began operations in 1992 .

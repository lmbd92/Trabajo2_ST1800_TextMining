@@38951514 ' ' The Checklist Manifesto ' ' is a 2009 non-fiction book by Atul Gawande . It was released on December 22 , 2009 through Metropolitan Books and focuses on the usage of checklists in relation to several different elements of daily and professional life . The book looks at the usage of checklists in the business world as well as the medical profession , with Gawande examining how it could be used for greater efficiency . Gawande stated that he was inspired to write ' ' The Checklist Manifesto ' ' after reading a story about a young child that survived a fall into a frozen pond and discovering that the physician that saved her relied heavily on checklists . Critical reception for the book has been mostly positive , with ' ' Newsday ' ' calling it thoughtfully written .

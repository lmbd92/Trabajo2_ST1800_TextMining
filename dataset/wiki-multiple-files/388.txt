It was during this case that the events that sparked the case of ' ' Hill v. The Church of Scientology itself was convicted on two counts of breach of the public trust : infiltration of the offices of @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ Attorney General . The Church of Scientology was ordered to pay a $250,000 fine . The Church of Scientology became the only Canadian religious organization to be convicted for breaching the public trust ( the term ' religious organization ' being used loosely ; the Church of Scientology is not officially recognised by the Canadian Government as a religion ) . # Appeal # The case was appealed in 1996 before the Court of Appeal for Ontario by the Church of Scientology and one of the individual defendants , Jacqueline Matz .

@@31714514 ' ' Age of the Joker ' ' is the ninth studio album by the German power metal band Edguy . It was released on 26 August 2011 through Nuclear Blast . Alongside the normal release , a double disc digipak edition was also issued , featuring b-sides , single versions of two album songs , as well as a cover of the Slade hit Cum on Feel the Noize . A video was shot for Robin Hood , featuring German comedian Bernhard Hoecker in a satirical take on ' ' The Adventures of Robin Hood ' ' movie . The album also features a return to the power metal style whereas the previous two albums focused more towards a heavy metal/hard rock oriented style .

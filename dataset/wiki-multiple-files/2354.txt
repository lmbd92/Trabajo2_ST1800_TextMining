Upon insistence of the French , M'ba allowed opposition candidates to run , which it claimed was the main reason for starting the coup in the first place . However , their leaders were barred from participating because of their involvement in the coup , and known anti-Mba organizers were deported to remote parts of the country . In addition , M'ba was known to have bribed voters with banknotes . Severe electoral irregularities were reported .

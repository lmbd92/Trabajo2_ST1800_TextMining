Recent development # According to Law act no 6360 , all Turkish provinces with a population more than 750 000 , were renamed as metropolitan municipality . Furthermore , the central district will be split into two and two new districts are established ; Yunusemre and ehzadeler . All districts in those provinces became second level municipalities and all villages in those districts were renamed as a neighborhoods . Thus the villages listed above are officially neighborhoods of Manisa.

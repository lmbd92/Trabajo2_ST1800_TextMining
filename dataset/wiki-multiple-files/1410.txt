@@8923514 birthplace = Rosario , Uruguay Fabio Zerpa ( born in Rosario , Uruguay ) is an actor , parapsychologist and UFO researcher . He resides in Argentina since 1951. # Biography # Fabio Zerpa arrived in Argentina in 1951 . Following a short theatrical career , he became increasingly interested in extraterrestrial life , having already studied psychology . After some years of investigation , Zerpa started to give his first conferences at the beginning of the 1960s . In 1966 he created the radio program ' ' Ms all de la cuarta dimensin ' ' ( Beyond the Fourth Dimension ) . Since then , Fabio Zerpa has reported on more than 3,000 cases of UFO sightings and contact with extraterrestrials . Since 2001 he has been the director of the on-line magazine ' ' El Quinto Hombre ' ' ( The Fifth Man ) .

@@114514 Walnut is a city in Crawford County , Kansas , United States . As of the 2010 census , the city population was 220. # History # Walnut was originally called Glenwood , and under the latter name laid out in 1871 . In 1874 , it was renamed Walnut after the Little Walnut Creek , on which it is located . The first post office at Walnut was established in April , 1873 . Prior to April , 1877 , the post office 's name was officially called Walnut Station . # Geography # Walnut is located at ( 37.600359 , -95.075867 ) .

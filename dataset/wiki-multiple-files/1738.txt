@@12649514 birthplace = Durham , ON , CAN Jeff MacMillan ( born March 30 , 1979 ) is a Canadian professional ice hockey player . At the height of his career he briefly played for the Dallas Stars of the National Hockey League . # Career # MacMillan began his career in 1996 , icing for the junior team the Oshawa Generals in the OHL . MacMillan stayed with the Generals for three years , making almost 170 appearances in that time . His solid defensive play combined with a reasonable offensive output for a defenceman meant that in the 1999 NHL entry draft , MacMillan was drafted in the seventh round by the Dallas Stars . MacMillan was sent to play for the Fort Wayne Komets in the UHL , where he played just seven games before being moved to the Michigan K-Wings , a Dallas Stars affiliate team playing in the IHL . In the K-Wings ' last season as a Stars affiliate , MacMillan featured in 53 games . MacMillan was a cornerstone of the Grizzlies team , and featured in over 300 games during his stay there . MacMillan 's hard work and natural defensive qualities meant that during the 2003/04 season he also featured for the Dallas Stars themselves , and played four regular season NHL games . The following season , MacMillan was again relegated back down to the AHL , this time playing for the Hartford Wolf Pack . In his one season playing for Hartford he was again a key player , playing 71 times and clocking up 136 penalty minutes . MacMillan would play one more season in the AHL in 2005/06 , for the Syracuse Crunch . MacMillan proved to be a solid , physical defender for the Phoenix but struggled to find form , suffering a number of injuries through the season . The Manchester defence was notably unreliable during the 2007/08 season and at the end of the season , head coach Hand made the decision to re-model the entire defence , releasing MacMillan . MacMillan and Fankhouser would again choose to sign for the same club , agreeing to play for the Bloomington Prairie Thunder of the United Hockey League , an announcement made in July 2008 . Since his professional days , MacMillan has split his time between the Durham Thundercats of the WOAA Senior AA Hockey League and the Dundas Real McCoys of Allan Cup Hockey .

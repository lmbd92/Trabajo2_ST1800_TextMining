@@37684514 Format = Music download Label = Arista Nashville Let the Cowboy Rock is a song co-written and recorded by American country music artist Ronnie Dunn . It was released in January 2012 as the third single from his self-titled album . Dunn wrote this song with Dallas Davidson . # History # While the song was charting , Dunn began soliciting fan suggestions for a fourth single on his Facebook page . He later posted on May 15 that Sony executives had called him and told him that the solicitations killed the Let The Cowboy Rock single . A month later , he posted that he had exited Arista Nashville .

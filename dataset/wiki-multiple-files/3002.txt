Despite his limited playing time in the majors , Duff 's minor league career spanned eight nonconsecutive seasons . # Early life # Duff was born in Providence , Rhode Island on May 6 , 1875 , though 1890 United States Census records suggests he was born in 1876 . His father , Patrick Duff , was born in Ireland in 1830 , and worked as a laborer . Duff 's mother was also from Ireland . Duff attended Manhattan College from 19011904. # Professional career # In 1897 , Duff began his professional career with the Class-B Fall River Indians of the New England League .

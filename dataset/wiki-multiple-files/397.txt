@@1281514 ' ' La Cenicienta ' ' ( ' ' Cinderella ' ' ) is an opera in three acts composed by Chilean artist Jorge Pea Hen ( 1928&amp;ndash ; 1973 ) , to a libretto by the Chilean author , Oscar Jara Azocar ( 1910&amp;ndash ; 1988 ) . This opera is one of the few operas in the world composed with the aim to be sung , acted and played exclusively by children . It was written in 1966 and played by Jorge Pea Hen 's first Symphonic Orchestra of Children at the Theater of the Girls Public School in La Serena , a city in the northern region of Coquimbo . In 1967 , a production of ' ' La Cenicienta ' ' went on a tour through several cities in Chile : Valparaso , Via del Mar , Concepcin and Santiago , the capital . Its debut was considered a great success . In 1986 , a new version of this children 's opera was played in Colombia .

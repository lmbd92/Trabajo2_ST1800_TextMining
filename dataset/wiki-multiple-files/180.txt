@@414514 Thomas Hodge Tom Allen ( born April 16 , 1945 ) is a former member of the United States House of Representatives representing , and the Democratic nominee for the U.S. Senate in 2008 against Republican incumbent Senator Susan Collins . Allen lost to Collins ( 61.5% to 38.5% ) . Allen was first elected in 1996 , defeating Republican incumbent James Longley , Jr . with 55 percent of votes cast to Longley 's 45 percent . Allen was re-elected five times , receiving over 55 percent of the vote each time in his district , until his defeat in his 2008 run for the U.S. Senate . After , Allen was appointed president and CEO of the Association of American Publishers and began his term on May 1 , 2009 .

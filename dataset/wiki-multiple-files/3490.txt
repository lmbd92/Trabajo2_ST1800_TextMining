@@32180514 country = Bosnia &amp; Herzegovina Austria ' ' Body Complete ' ' is a 2012 film with an international cast by the producers Robert Hofferer and Lukas Sturm as well as the co-producer Puls 4 from 2011 . Lukas Sturm directed the film and also wrote the script . Asli Bayram plays the leading role . # Background # The film deals with the impact of the Bosnian war on society . The so-called ethnic cleansing during this cruel war in the center of Europe happened in the sphere of influence of two men : former politician Radovan Karadi , accused of being a war criminal , and former general Ratko Mladi , who was caught during the shooting of this film . Mladi is being held responsible for the mass murder of at least 8,000 unarmed Bosnian civilians in Srebrenica in July 1995 . The story of the film is set in this historical background . The filming mainly took place at authentic settings in Sarajevo and its surroundings .

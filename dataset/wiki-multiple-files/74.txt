@@133514 Herndon , formerly Trevorton Junction , is a borough in Northumberland County , Pennsylvania , United States . Herndon was first founded by Robert A. Parrish in 1840 . The town was later named for Naval commander William Lewis Herndon , who died in 1857 while trying to rescue passengers from his sinking ship .

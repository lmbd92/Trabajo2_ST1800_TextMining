@@5685514 birthplace = Tappan , New York , U.S. Michael Rispoli ( born November 27 , 1960 ) is an American character actor . He was formerly part of the HBO television series ' ' The Sopranos ' ' as Jackie Aprile , Sr. Rispoli soon thereafter reunited with ' ' The Sopranos ' ' co-star James Gandolfini in the 2009 thriller ' ' The Taking of Pelham 1 2 3 ' ' . Rispoli was runner-up for the role of Tony Soprano . However , he lost it to Gandolfini . Rispoli , a second-generation Italian American , was born in Tappan , New York , one of eight children . He attended State University of New York at Plattsburgh as a Theater major and graduated in 1982 . In 2011 , the feature film ' ' Union Square ' ' , co-written and directed by the Sundance Film Festival 's Grand Jury Award Winner , Nancy Savoca , premiered at the Toronto Film Festival .

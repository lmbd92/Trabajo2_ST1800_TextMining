@@10771514 runtime = 153 minutes ' ' Amanush ' ' ( English:Inhuman ) is a 1975 Indian action drama film made in both Hindi and Bengali produced and directed by Shakti Samanta . The film stars Sharmila Tagore , Uttam Kumar , Utpal Dutt and Asit Sen. The Bengali version of the film achieved immense popularity amongst Bengalis and featured many memorable songs by Kishore Kumar like Bipinbabur karansudha and Ki asha-y baandhi khelaghar . Also , this film featured the Bengali film star Uttam Kumar at the height of his popularity and Utpal Dutt turned in a typically stellar performance as the villain .

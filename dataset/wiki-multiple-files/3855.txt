@@36628514 genre = Action role-playing is a Nintendo Family Computer action role-playing video game that was released exclusively in Japan on November 13 , 1987. # Story # An advanced civilization once flourished in a fictional solar system . One day , a black hole appeared and sucked everyone who lived there into another dimension . This parallel universe came to be known as ' ' Artelius ' ' . Ruled by the diabolical king Sarbelor , he controls a machine that has split the universe into spaces filled with asteroid belts . A cyborg has been built by a scientist named Dr. Rado in order to defeat Sarbelor ; only he has been entrusted with this mission to save Artelius from the evil king 's reign . # Gameplay # Players begin the game by customizing and modifying the traits of their character . Warp points allow players to travel across the universe . On the map , the red dots are always bad guys while the blue dots indicate space colonies , where players can rest and stock up on supplies .

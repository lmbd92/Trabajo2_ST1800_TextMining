@@21096514 James Sterling Corum is an American air power historian and scholar of counter-insurgency . He has written several books on counterinsurgency and other topics . He is a retired lieutenant colonel in the US Army Reserve . # Career # # Academic # Corum has been dean of the Baltic Defence College in Tartu , Estonia since January 2009 . He is also adjunct professor of military history at Austin Peay State University . He was formerly a professor of military history in the Department of Joint and Multinational Operations at the US Army Command and General Staff College at Fort Leavenworth . Previously he was professor of Comparative Military Studies at the school of advanced airpower studies , Air University , Alabama .

Her underlings include her right-hand man Ko Sau ( Andy Lau ) , nicknamed Judge . During a mission , Ko Sau meets a fellow newcomer Yu Fung ( Anita Yuen ) . During that time , he develops feelings for her . However , due to his lonely personality and the principles of an assassin career , it stalls the relationship . Despite this , they are still able to spend happy times together . During another mission , Ko Sau fails to complete the task when he saves a child .

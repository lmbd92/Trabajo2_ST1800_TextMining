@@15805514 position = Central defender Maxwell Stuart Max Thompson ( born 31 December 1956 ) is an English former footballer . A defender , he scored eight goals in 137 league games in a ten-year career in the Football League . At age 17 years and 128 days , he became Liverpool 's youngest ever player ( his record has since been broken ) when he made his debut in May 1974 . He joined Blackpool three years later , and went on to play 99 league games for the club in a four-year spell , and was also loaned out to Dallas Tornado and Seattle Sounders . He then spent the 1980s with various clubs across the world : Bournemouth , Port Vale , Baltimore Blast ( USA ) , Coimbra ( Portugal ) , Northwich Victoria , Caernarfon Town , Fleetwood , Newport County , Kramfors ( Sweden ) , and Southport .

Construction of the tower began in 2006 and the tower was finished on September 18 , 2008 . It is the tallest office building constructed in San Francisco since the 1980s . The building is the part of a new generation of skyscrapers in San Francisco 's downtown built on Mission Street since 2000 , including The Paramount , the St. Regis Museum Tower , Millennium Tower , 101 Second Street , and JP MorganChase Building . # History # The tower was originally approved by the Planning Commission on April 5 , 2001 to rise 137.5 m and 30 stories . A revised plan , adding three stories and raising the height to 147 m , was approved on December 13 , 2001 . However , due to the downturn in the office market after the dot-com bust , developer Tishman Speyer froze the project waiting for economic conditions to improve .

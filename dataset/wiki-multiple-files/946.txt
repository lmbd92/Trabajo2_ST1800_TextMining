@@4835514 Run to the Sun is a song by British synthpop duo Erasure , released as the second single from their sixth studio album ' ' I Say I Say I Say ' ' . An uptempo dance music track , the song displays signature synthesizer programming by band member Vince Clarke . Run to the Sun was written by Clarke with fellow Erasure member Andy Bell . The track peaked at number six on the UK singles chart ( Erasure 's fifteenth UK Top 10 hit ) . In Germany , Run to the Sun stalled at number forty-nine and in the U.S. it reached position twenty-four on the Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles chart . On the U.S. Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart , it climbed to number fourteen . The UK 7 single of Run to the Sun was issued on yellow-coloured vinyl and featured a fold-out poster of the single 's cover artwork .

@@4560514 Nancy McArthur is an American children 's author . Living in Berea , Ohio , she is a part-time journalism professor at Baldwin-Wallace College . She has written fourteen books , nine of which form a series called ' ' The Plant That Ate Dirty Socks ' ' . The series follows the lives of two young brothers , Michael and Norman , along with their sentient pet plants , Stanley and Fluffy . The first book in this series is the most popular book she has written .

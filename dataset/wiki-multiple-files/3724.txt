@@35034514 The 14th World Junior Championships in Athletics was an international athletics competition for athletes qualifying as juniors ( born 1993 or later ) which was held at the Estadi Olmpic Llus Companys in Barcelona , Spain , on 1015 July 2012 . A total of 44 athletics events were contested at the championships , 22 by male and 22 by female athletes . Several medalists from the 2010 championships were eligible to defend their titles , including Jacko Gill , Jodie Williams , Shaunae Miller , and Angelica Bengtsson .

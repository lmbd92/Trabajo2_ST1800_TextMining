He is under management of ABS-CBNs Star Magic talents . # Background # Dominic Roque was born and raised in the province of Cavite . He is the son of businessman and a company president . He is also a nephew of known actress Beth Tamayo . He attended college in De La SalleCollege of Saint Benilde and took Tourism course . # Career # Roque began modeling and endorsing several companies before headed into acting . He was a model for Cosmopolitan talents since 2008 , where he modeled the Philippine clothing brand , Penshoppe . In 2010 , Roque debuted onscreen in the soap opera casting Habang May Buhay playing Mark . He later took his first major role in the fantasy-drama series , Aryana in 2012 .

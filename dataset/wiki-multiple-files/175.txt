' ' **28;98810;TOOLONG NOTOC Kodzko County ( ) is a unit of territorial administration and local government ( powiat ) in Lower Silesian Voivodeship , south-western Poland . It came into being on January 1 , 1999 , as a result of the Polish local government reforms passed in 1998 . Its area roughly corresponds to the former German ' ' Grafschaft Glatz ' ' . The county covers an area of 1643km2 sqmi . Its administrative seat is the town of Kodzko ; it also contains the towns of Duszniki-Zdrj , Nowa Ruda , Polanica-Zdrj , Bystrzyca Kodzka , Kudowa-Zdrj , Ldek-Zdrj , Midzylesie , Radkw , Stronie lskie and Szczytna . ( The suffix ' ' Zdrj ' ' appearing in several of these names means spa . ) As of 2006 the total population of the county was 166,447 , of which the population of the towns was 108,262 and the rural population was 58,185. # Neighbouring counties # Kodzko County is bordered by Wabrzych County to the north-west , Dzieroniw County @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ .

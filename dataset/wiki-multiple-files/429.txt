It is made up of 11 vignettes , all revolving around a bar and brothel owned by Lady Sally McGee , wife of Mike Callahan . The stories are written in the same fast-paced , pun-laced prose Robinson is noted for . # Plot # The storyline focuses on Maureen , an underage streetwalker who is stabbed by her pimp in a dark alley one night . Fortunately for Maureen , Lady Sally is nearby : she had been walking her werebeagle . After defeating the pimp in hand to hand combat , Sally brings Maureen home . Her brothel has a fully functioning medical facility , where Maureen is treated and healed . She falls in love with the place for its ambiance and safety but is rejected as an employee for many reasons . Most importantly , she is underage but she also has severe self-loathing issues .

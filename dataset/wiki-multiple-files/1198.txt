@@6859514 birthplace=Hugginstown , County Kilkenny , Ireland John Tennyson ( born 6 February 1985 ) is an Irish hurler who played as a centre-back for the Kilkenny senior team . Born in Mabbotstown near Hugginstown , County Kilkenny , Tennyson first excelled at hurling whilst at school in St. Kieran 's College . He arrived on the inter-county scene at the age of seventeen when he first linked up with the Kilkenny minor team , before later lining out with the under-21 side . He made his senior debut in the 2005 championship . Tennyson went on to play for what has come to be known as the greatest team of all-time , and has won three All-Ireland medals , four Leinster medals and two National League medals . His brother , Pat Tennyson , was also an All-Ireland medallist with Kilkenny . Tennyson represented the Leinster inter-provincial team on a number of occasions , winning his sole Inter-provincial medal in 2006 . At club level he continues to play for Carrickshock .

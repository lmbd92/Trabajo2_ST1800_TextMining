The selected colors are cyan , magenta , yellow , black and white . The device has ' uni colored ' body rather than combination . The 206 's back design is extremely similar to the Nokia Lumia 920. # Camera # The device is able to capture pictures at resolution of 1280 x 960 pixels with a 1.3-megapixel camera .

@@39055514 Steven Edward Drobny ( born April 29 , 1972 ) is an American hedge fund advisor and published author . He is the founder of Drobny Capital , a consulting and investment advisory business focused on fundamental discretionary global macro and commodity hedge fund strategies . Drobny is the author of ' ' The Invisible Hands : Top Hedge Funds on Bubbles , Crashes and Rethinking Real Money ( John Wiley &amp; Sons , 2010 ) ' ' and ' ' Inside the House of Money : Top Hedge Fund Traders on Profiting in the Global Markets ( John Wiley &amp; Sons , 2006 ) ' ' # Education and career # Drobny attended Bucknell University , graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration . Steven Drobny founded Drobny Capital in 2007 and leads the firm 's asset management , consulting and investment advisory business .

@@33680514 Hush Little Baby is a song by British rapper Wretch 32 which appears on his debut studio album , ' ' Black and White ' ' . The song is track thirteen on the standard edition of the album , and one of eleven collaborations ; currently set to be released as the fifth and final single from the album on 27 May 2012 . The song , which features singer-songwriter Ed Sheeran , was produced by the team TMS and co-written alongside Iain James . The track debuted at number eighty-seven on the UK Singles Chart on 3 September 2011 following strong downloading from the respective album . The song is an adaptation of the American lullaby , ' ' Hush , Little Baby ' ' .
